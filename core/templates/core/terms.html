3. Description of Service

Rewright is an AI-powered writing tool designed to rewrite AI-generated text so that it reads more naturally and sounds more human-written. The Service provides two distinct processing modes:

Quick Fix Mode: A rule-based processing engine that identifies and corrects common patterns associated with AI-generated writing. This mode operates entirely on our servers using pattern-matching algorithms and does not rely on third-party artificial intelligence services. It is available only for English-language text.

Deep Rewrite Mode: An AI-powered rewriting engine that sends your text to third-party large language model (LLM) providers for processing. Currently, the Service uses OpenAI's GPT-4o model. We may also use Anthropic's Claude models in the future. When a provider change occurs, we will update this section of the Terms accordingly. Deep Rewrite mode supports multiple languages and offers tone selection and voice calibration features.

The Service also provides optional features including voice calibration (matching your personal writing style), tone selection (casual, formal, academic, simple, summarize, expand), downloadable output files, session-based rewrite history, and personal analytics dashboards.

7. Text Processing and Data Handling

When you submit text for rewriting, your text is processed as follows:

Quick Fix Mode: Your text is processed entirely on our servers using our rule-based pattern engine. The text is held in memory during processing and is not permanently stored in our database. No third-party services receive your text in this mode.

Deep Rewrite Mode: Your text is sent to third-party AI providers (currently OpenAI; potentially Anthropic in the future) via their secure APIs for processing. These providers process your text according to their own terms of service, privacy policies, and data handling practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these providers. We send only the text content necessary for rewriting. No personal identifying information (such as your username, email, or account details) is included in API requests to these providers.

In both modes, we log metadata about your rewrite requests for analytics and service improvement purposes. This metadata includes: the processing mode used, tone selected, detected language, input and output word counts, input character count, and timestamp. For guest users, we also log the IP address for usage tracking purposes. We do not permanently store the actual text content you submit for rewriting or the rewritten output in our database.

If you use the voice calibration feature, your writing sample is sent to the third-party AI provider along with the text to be rewritten. The writing sample is not permanently stored on our servers.
